Game Instructions

> <

  • Below is a display of both yours and your partner's screens. Both screens have the same map background but different obstacles overlaid. There is one ball and one goal between the two of you.


  • When you play, this will be your view. Just as you cannot see your partner's screen, they
    cannot see yours. Your screens will most likely be very different.





  • You can chat with your partner to exchange information and strategize.

  • You can also place blocks on your partner's screen by pressing Ctrl, but only three at a time.





  • If this message pops up, it means that if placed, the block would be sitting on the ball.









    A similar message will appear if it would hit your partner. To fix this, either
    place your block elsewhere or ask your partner to move themselves or the ball.


  • This is a teleporter      

    There will be one in your screen and one in your partner's screen. Teleporters will
    transport the ball between your screen and your partner's screen.
    Press the full-screen button on the video below to see how a teleporter works.




  • If absolutely stuck on a level or if your block is stuck, you can hit reset. Hitting reset will
    only reset the current level you are on. All blocks that have been placed will be removed
    (or will not function anymore).







  • The key to this game is communication,
    so make sure you are communicating effectively!


  • Now, hit refresh and click on the "Two-Player Tutorial"!
