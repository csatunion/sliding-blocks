How to play

  • Push the ball into the goal.
  • Use the arrow keys to move around.
  • Use CTRL to place a block on your partners screen.
  • Use the chat area on the left to send messages to your partner.
  • Hint: You and your partner see the same background image, but different mazes.
  • Hint: If the ball is stuck on a wall, push it into the wall to help get it unstuck.
  • Hint: You can only place 3 blocks on your partners screen at once. When you drop the 4th block the 1st block will disappear.
