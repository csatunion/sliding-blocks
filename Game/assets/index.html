How to play

Goal: push the ball into the goal.
Use the arrow keys to move around. Press the Control key to place a block on your partners screen.
Note that both you and your partner see the same background screen.
Hint: If the ball is stuck on a wall, push it into the wall to help get it unstuck.
Hint: You can only place 3 blocks on your partners screen at once. When you drop the 4th block the 1st block will disappear.
